About Us:

Welcome to Stampley's Salmon Cookie shop! My name is Aunri Stampley and I am the owner of Stampley's Cookie shop. For as long as I can remember, I've always loved to bake! Due to my passion of baking and being unorthodoxed, we've decided to put the two together and create some of the most out of this world desserts and pastries! We are most known for our Salmon cookies, don't knock it til you try it! Come check us out at one of our locations!
